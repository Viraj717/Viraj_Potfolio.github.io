Personal Project · Unreal Engine 5 · Prototype
Zombie Shooter
Prototype

An intense zombie-survival prototype built in Unreal Engine 5. Set within a confined cell, the player faces relentless waves of zombies multiplying with each round. Armed with an auto-gun with unlimited ammo — defend the gate separating you from the undead horde.

The gate serves as the final barrier, constantly under attack. While mowing down threats, you must repair the gate to delay the onslaught. Once breached, the zombies swarm and end the game — how long can you survive?
