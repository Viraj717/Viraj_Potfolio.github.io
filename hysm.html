The Project
About the Game

This project was developed in collaboration with Cosmic Sabbath, a U.K.-based independent game studio. It marks the studio's first full release and my first shipped title as part of a professional team.

Have You Seen This Man? is a narrative-driven psychological horror game set in 1970s Texas. Players step into Emsdorf County, a quiet rural town hiding unspeakable secrets, as they uncover a chilling story of paranoia, fear, and survival. The game is the first chapter in Cosmic Sabbath's American Hell anthology series.

  • First-person narrative horror gameplay
  • Immersive environmental storytelling
  • Dynamic lighting and atmospheric tone
  • Exploration and dialogue-driven interactions
  • Psychological horror narrative grounded in realism
