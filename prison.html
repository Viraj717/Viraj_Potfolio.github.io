The Game
About This Project

Immerse yourself in the gripping world of Prison Escape 3D — a thrilling stealth game meticulously crafted in the Unity Engine. Navigate through treacherous dark dungeons as a captive, utilizing stealth and strategy to evade capture and secure your freedom.

Forge alliances with cunning infiltrator guards, trading valuable resources for vital assistance in your quest for liberation. Every decision matters as you plan your escape route through the prison's maze-like corridors.
