The World
About This Project

This prototype presents a hauntingly abandoned city, where the echoes of civilization linger in decayed structures and desolate roads. Overrun by nature, the once-thriving streets now lie in silence, with only the wind and distant creaks of crumbling buildings breaking the void.

Created in Unreal Engine 5, this environment showcases intricate level design and atmospheric storytelling, immersing players in a world reclaimed by time and neglect.
